Recognizing Global Reservoirs From Landsat 8 Images: A Deep Learning Approach
Weizhen Fang, Cunguang Wang, Xi Chen, Wei Wan, Huan Li, Siyu Zhu, Yu Fang, Baojian Liu, Yang Hong
IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing, 2019

Online interactive platform

Man-made reservoirs are key components of terrestrial hydrological systems.Here we established a Global Reservoir and Lake Training database clipped from moderate resolution Landsat 8 images for using deep learning method to identify waterbody's type. The database is of free access for scientific and educational applications.
